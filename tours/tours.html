    Custom Walking Tours

    Our most popular option, these walking tours cover a variety of topics and Park sites. Learn the inside scoop on the Park's history and design, landscape architecture, most popular sites, and many other features that make Central Park so unique.

    Tour with Central Park Conservancy Historian and Photographer

    Learn stories and secrets from the Park's amazing history, and appreciate some little-known landscapes and sculptures on this tour with Sara Cedar Miller, Central Park Conservancy's official historian and photographer for thirty years.

    Learn More

    Sara's work has been featured in publications and television around the world. Her bestselling books include Central Park, An American Masterpiece; Seeing Central Park; and Strawberry Fields. You will receive one of these as a thank you for taking Sara's custom tour.

    • Driving or walking tours throughout the year; weekdays only
    • Up to 60 minutes: starting at $500
    • Up to 90 minutes: starting at $750
    • Up to 2 hours: starting at $1000
    • Walking tours available for individuals and groups of up to 20 people
    • Driving tours available for individuals and groups up to 6 people
    • Please request your tour at least 3 weeks in advance

    Conservatory Garden Tour with Garden Curators

    Tour the Park's only formal garden, known for its extraordinary floral displays, through the eyes of its curator and director Diane Schaub, and her experienced staff.

    Learn More

    With over twenty years of experience managing and designing for the Garden, Diane offers a singular perspective on its history and design, as well as some of the special plantings found in this six-acre urban gem. Hundreds of annual and perennial varieties, eighty-year old crabapple allées, and exceptional fountain sculptures are among the Garden's treasures.

    • Tours available April through November; weekdays only
    • Up to 90 minutes: starting at $250.00
    • Available for individuals and groups up to 40 people
    • Discounts available for seniors, students, not-for-profit organizations, and members of Central Park Conservancy
    • Please request your tour at least 3 weeks in advance

    Additional Information

    Custom Tour Hours:
    Custom tours are available seven days per week, 9:00 am - 6:00 pm (9:00 am - 3:00 pm during winter months), based on availability.

    Booking and Payment:
    Please submit a Booking Request Form to schedule a custom tour. A Central Park Conservancy staff member will contact you within 3 business days to customize and schedule your tour. A reservation deposit will be required to secure the tour at that time. Your credit card will be charged the full amount upon completion of your custom tour. We accept American Express, MasterCard, and Visa.

    Cancellation Policy for Custom Tours:
    Tours take place rain or shine, however, they may be cancelled in the case of extreme weather conditions. In the event of a weather cancellation, the tour may be rescheduled.

    Deposits are non-refundable if requesting party cancels after the tour has been confirmed.

    For questions, contact us at: tours@centralparknyc.org or 212.360.2726.
