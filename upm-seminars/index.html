The Urban Park Management Seminar Series is a regular gathering for the New York City parks community offered by the Central Park Conservancy Institute for Urban Parks through the Center for Urban Park Management. We host these conversations to provide an opportunity for other park groups to learn more about what we do here at Central Park Conservancy and to provide a forum for learning from one another.
